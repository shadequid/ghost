# What is Ghost?

Ghost is an AI companion for Hyperliquid perpetual contract traders. Not a dashboard. Not a bot. A companion that helps you manage risk, maintain discipline, and trade with emotional awareness.

## What Ghost is NOT

- **Not a trading bot** — Ghost won't execute trades without your permission. You decide when to trade; Ghost reminds you why.
- **Not a dashboard clone** — Ghost isn't another way to view your positions. You already have Hyperliquid for that.
- **Not a generic AI chatbot** — Ghost speaks trading, not general knowledge. Every feature solves a real trader's problem.
- **Not a backtesting suite** — Ghost doesn't replay your past. It helps you decide better in the present.

## Who Ghost is for

**Marcus, 27** — Has a clear trading strategy but loses discipline. Wins a few trades, gets overconfident, raises leverage from 5x to 15x, forgets SL, then takes a 35% loss. Ghost reminds him when leverage creeps up, calls out behavioral drift before he presses the button, and consolidates everything into one place so he stops switching between 6 apps.

**Kevin, 32** — Runs a business; trading is side income. Gets liquidated while in meetings because he couldn't manage positions remotely. Ghost lets him check PnL or close a trade in 10 seconds via Telegram—no app required. When bad news hits, Ghost monitors even when Kevin is busy and alerts immediately.

**Elena, 25** — Quant background, spends 2 hours daily aggregating data from 5 sources, scrolls X constantly. Analyzes thoroughly but hesitates to enter, then cuts profits too early from anxiety. Ghost consolidates OI, funding, whale activity, and news in one place, gives her a clear yes/no to reduce analysis paralysis, and reminds her to hold stops to plan.

## What makes Ghost different

- **Pre-trade advisory** — Before you place an order, Ghost evaluates: Is funding unsustainably high? Are whales bailing? Did you just lose? This happens at the moment it matters, not in a journal after the trade.
- **Emotion-aware** — Ghost detects FOMO, revenge trading, and overconfidence by comparing your current order to your history. It calls the pattern by name, shows you the damage from last time, and asks you to reconsider.
- **Proactive observer** — Ghost watches your positions 24/7. When funding eating into profit, when liquidation distance closes, when whale moves on your position—Ghost tells you immediately, not 2 hours later.
- **Paper-trading-first** — Start with $10,000 simulated capital. Trade real decisions, learn at zero cost, move to live when you're ready.

## When NOT to use Ghost

- **Spot traders only** — Ghost lives on Hyperliquid perp DEX. If you trade BTC/USDT on Binance, Ghost can't help you yet.
- **Non-Hyperliquid exchanges** — Aster DEX and others coming soon; today it's Hyperliquid-only.
- **Full-auto bot users** — If you want Ghost to trade without permission, run stop-losses automatically, or execute on signals—that's not Ghost. Set up a bot instead.
- **Zero slippage requirement** — Ghost uses market orders when you ask for speed. If you need precise fills, place limit orders yourself.
