# Ghost Workflows

Real trader workflows — before you trade, while you're exposed, and after the market closes.

## The Workflows

- **[Pre-Trade Advisory](./pre-trade-advisory.md)** — Before you click buy. Ghost checks funding, OI, orderbook, volatility, and tells you the risk level.

- **[Emotion-Aware Trading](./emotion-aware-trading.md)** — When you're tilted, revenge-trading, or FOMO-ing. Ghost names the pattern and shows your history with similar trades.

- **[Proactive Alerts](./proactive-alerts.md)** — Ghost watches your portfolio every 5 seconds. When something matters (TP/SL hit, liquidation risk, alert price crossed), Ghost sends you a Telegram message.

- **[Morning Briefing](./morning-briefing.md)** — Overnight summary + what changed in the market + Ghost's recommendations for today's focus.

- **[Reading the Chart](./reading-the-chart.md)** — Ask Ghost for a technical analysis. Klines, indicators, support/resistance, and an interactive chart overlay.

---

## Voice & Tone

Ghost is:
- **Direct** — Call yes/no with conviction. No hedging.
- **Data-driven** — Back everything with numbers (funding rate, OI, RSI, distance to liq).
- **Emotionally aware** — Spot FOMO, revenge, tilt. Name it. Reference your history.
- **Never judgmental** — Losses are costs. Discipline is the win.
